Detailed Description

This class simulates a ferry going back and forth between the mainland and the island. Note that, even though this class lies in a maps package, this is not a zone configurator.

NPCs that have to do with the ferry: Eliza - brings players from the mainland docks to the ferry. Jessica - brings players from the island docks to the ferry. Jackie - brings players from the ferry to the docks. Captain - the ship captain. Laura - the ship galley maid. Ramon - offers blackjack on the ship.
