Detailed Description

A gold source is a spot where a player can prospect for gold nuggets. He needs a gold pan, time, and luck.

Prospecting takes 7-11 seconds; during this time, the player keep standing next to the gold source. In fact, the player only has to be there when the prospecting action has finished. Therefore, make sure that two gold sources are always at least 5 sec of walking away from each other, so that the player can't prospect for gold at several sites simultaneously.

Some karma is used to decide if the player was successful at the well or not.
