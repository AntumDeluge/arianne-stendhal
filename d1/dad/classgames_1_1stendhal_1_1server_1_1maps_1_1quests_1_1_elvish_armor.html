Detailed Description

QUEST: ElvishArmor

PARTICIPANTS:

  • Lupos, an albino elf who live in Fado Forest

STEPS:

  • Lupos wants to see every piece of elvish equipment you can bring him

REWARD:

  • 20000 XP
  • Karma:25
  • ability to sell elvish stuff and also drow sword

REPETITIONS:

  • None.
