Detailed Description

QUEST: CleanStorageSpace

PARTICIPANTS: Eonna

STEPS: Eonna asks you to clean her storage space. You go kill at least a rat, a cave rat and a cobra. Eonna checks your kills and then thanks you.

REWARD: 100 XP, karma

REPETITIONS: None.
