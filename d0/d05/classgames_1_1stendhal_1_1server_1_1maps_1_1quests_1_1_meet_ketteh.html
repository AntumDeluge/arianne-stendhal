Detailed Description

QUEST: Speak with Ketteh

PARTICIPANTS: - Ketteh Wehoh, a woman

STEPS: - Talk to Ketteh to activate the quest and keep speaking with Ketteh.

REWARD: - No XP - No money

REPETITIONS: - As much as wanted.
